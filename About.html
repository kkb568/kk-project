SERVICES OFFERED

We offer tourists(both local and international tourists) transport and touring services to national parks and reserves in Kenya.

The touring involves taking the tourists inside the desired park/reserve, with the help of tourist guides.

In addition, we show the various hotels that are near the desired park or reserve.
This can be viewed in the booking form page of the desired park or reserve under the 'Services' page.
